# Movies on Demand - User Features

**User-facing feature description from the user's perspective. This document describes what users can do and what the feature provides.**

## Overview

A personal movie discovery and information utility that allows users to search for movies, browse popular and trending films, view detailed movie information including ratings, cast, and plot summaries, and explore movie recommendations. Users can discover movies from The Movie Database (TMDB) and access comprehensive information about films they're interested in watching.

## Key Features

### Movie Search

Users can search for movies by title, allowing them to quickly find specific films they're looking for. The search provides instant results as they type, showing movie posters, titles, release dates, and ratings.

### Browse Popular Movies

Users can browse a curated list of currently popular movies, helping them discover what's trending and what others are watching. This provides a quick way to find new content to watch.

### Browse Trending Movies

Users can view movies that are currently trending, which may differ from popular movies based on recent activity and engagement. This helps users stay up-to-date with what's hot right now.

### Movie Details

Users can view comprehensive information about any movie, including:

-   Movie poster and backdrop images
-   Title, release date, and runtime
-   Overview/plot summary
-   Genres
-   Rating and vote count
-   Cast and crew information
-   Production companies
-   Budget and revenue information

### Movie Recommendations

Users can discover similar movies based on films they're viewing, helping them find new content that matches their interests.

## User Workflows

### Search for a Movie

**Goal**: Find a specific movie by name

**Steps**:

1. Navigate to the movies on demand page
2. Enter a movie title in the search box
3. View search results as they appear
4. Click on a movie to view its details

**Result**: Movie information is displayed with all available details

### Browse Popular Movies

**Goal**: Discover popular movies to watch

**Steps**:

1. Navigate to the movies on demand page
2. Click on "Popular Movies" section
3. Browse through the list of popular movies
4. Click on any movie to view its details

**Result**: Access to a curated list of popular movies with detailed information

### Browse Trending Movies

**Goal**: See what movies are trending right now

**Steps**:

1. Navigate to the movies on demand page
2. Click on "Trending Movies" section
3. Browse through the list of trending movies
4. Click on any movie to view its details

**Result**: Access to currently trending movies with detailed information

### View Movie Details

**Goal**: Get comprehensive information about a movie

**Steps**:

1. Navigate to the movies on demand page
2. Search for a movie or browse popular/trending lists
3. Click on a movie card or title
4. View the movie details page with all available information

**Result**: Complete movie information including cast, crew, ratings, and plot summary

### Discover Similar Movies

**Goal**: Find movies similar to one you're viewing

**Steps**:

1. Navigate to a movie's details page
2. Scroll to the "Similar Movies" section
3. Browse the list of recommended similar films
4. Click on any similar movie to view its details

**Result**: Discovery of new movies that match your viewing preferences

## User Capabilities

-   Search for movies by title with instant results
-   Browse popular movies curated by TMDB
-   Browse trending movies based on current activity
-   View detailed movie information including:
    -   Posters and backdrop images
    -   Plot summaries and overviews
    -   Cast and crew details
    -   Ratings and reviews
    -   Genre classifications
    -   Production information
-   Discover similar movies based on current selection
-   Filter and sort movie lists
-   View movie metadata (release date, runtime, budget, revenue)

## Use Cases

### Movie Discovery

Find new movies to watch by browsing popular and trending lists, or by exploring similar movies based on films you're interested in.

### Movie Research

Look up detailed information about movies before watching them, including ratings, cast, plot summaries, and production details.

### Quick Movie Lookup

Search for specific movies by name to quickly find information, ratings, and details about films you're considering watching.

### Content Planning

Browse popular and trending movies to stay current with what's popular and plan your viewing schedule.

## User Benefits

-   **Comprehensive Information**: Access detailed movie data from TMDB's extensive database
-   **Easy Discovery**: Browse popular and trending movies to find new content
-   **Fast Search**: Quick movie lookup with instant search results
-   **Visual Interface**: Movie posters and images help identify films quickly
-   **No Account Required**: Browse and search movies without needing to sign up
-   **Up-to-Date**: Access current popular and trending movie lists
-   **Similar Recommendations**: Discover new movies based on your interests
