# Video Call - User Features

**User-facing feature description from the user's perspective. This document describes what users can do and what the feature provides.**

## Overview

A video conferencing utility that allows users to join video calls directly from their browser. Users can see and hear other participants in real-time, with controls for camera and microphone.

## Key Features

### Join Video Calls

Users can join video calls instantly by navigating to the video call page. No downloads or installations required - everything works in the browser.

### See Multiple Participants

All participants are displayed in a grid layout, making it easy to see everyone in the call at once. The layout adjusts automatically based on the number of participants.

### Camera and Microphone Controls

Users can toggle their camera and microphone on or off during the call. Controls are easily accessible and provide immediate feedback.

### Real-Time Video and Audio

High-quality video and audio streaming in real-time, with low latency for natural conversation flow.

### Browser-Based

No software installation required. Everything works directly in the web browser, making it accessible from any device.

## User Workflows

### Join a Video Call

**Goal**: Start or join a video call with others

**Steps**:
1. Navigate to the video call page
2. Allow camera and microphone permissions when prompted
3. Automatically connect to the video call room
4. See yourself and other participants in the grid layout
5. Start talking!

**Result**: User is in an active video call with other participants

### Toggle Camera During Call

**Goal**: Turn camera on or off during a call

**Steps**:
1. During an active call, locate the camera control button
2. Click to toggle camera on/off
3. Video feed updates immediately

**Result**: Camera is enabled or disabled as desired

### Toggle Microphone During Call

**Goal**: Mute or unmute microphone during a call

**Steps**:
1. During an active call, locate the microphone control button
2. Click to toggle microphone on/off
3. Audio status updates immediately

**Result**: Microphone is muted or unmuted as desired

### Leave a Video Call

**Goal**: Exit the video call

**Steps**:
1. During an active call, click the "Leave" button
2. Camera and microphone are automatically turned off
3. User is disconnected from the call

**Result**: User has left the video call

## User Capabilities

-   Join video calls instantly from any browser
-   See multiple participants in a grid layout
-   Toggle camera on/off during calls
-   Toggle microphone on/off during calls
-   See participant names and status
-   Leave calls at any time
-   No account or installation required

## Use Cases

### Remote Meetings

Conduct video meetings with team members, clients, or collaborators from anywhere with internet access.

### Social Calls

Video chat with friends and family members, seeing everyone together in one place.

### Quick Check-Ins

Join brief video calls for quick conversations without the overhead of scheduling or setup.

### Group Discussions

Have group video conversations with multiple participants, seeing everyone's reactions and expressions in real-time.
